OUR MISSION

GPTC Attingal is under the department of Technical Education Kerala.We believes in system of quality education.
They want to declare that this institution is imparting quality education with the social concerns.At present the college proudly offers Diploma courses and certificate courses in Technical subjects like Computer Hardware Maintanance,Automobile Engineering,Mechanical Engineering and Electrical & Electronics Engineering.
The administrative block where the office, Principal’s Room, Class Rooms and some of the labs are functioning here. Apart from the main block, there are four workshop/laboratory buildings in the campus.

Government Polytechnic College, Attingal has a well rounded team of faculty staffs with faculty members . They are industry experts and proven leaders in their field being engaged in boundary-extending research on key management issues.
